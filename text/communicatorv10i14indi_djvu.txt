all of the employees at IPFW that oil taxpayers will 
be able to double their income tax credit for con-

The tax credit for an individual filing a single return 
is 50% of all contributions up to $200. For individuals 
filing a joint return, the tax credit is now 50% of all 
contributions up to $400. 

Donations can be for unrestricted purposes, or they 
can be designoted for a particular department or 
program. To receive tax credit for 1978, checks must 
be dated by December 31 , 1978.
